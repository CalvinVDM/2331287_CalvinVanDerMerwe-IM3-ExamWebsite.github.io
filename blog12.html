BLOG POST 12

Week 12 - Internet, Society and Design Justice / Web APIs

“What’s wrong with this picture” was surprising, to say the least I never really thought how unethical some code can be and how something can look or be innocent in nature can result in something so outrageous and offensive. I understand that code is not sentient and that it just does what it is told but that means that it is on us as coders and programmers to make sure that errors like these are corrected and never take place again.

When looking at situations like these one realises that we must be more mindful and gain deeper understanding and consideration when we make things so that we don’t offend people unintentionally because I imagine that these situations aren’t always intentional and that sometimes it is simply by human error that these situations are developed and come out. This is why inclusive design is important and that we make sure we practice it so that we can respect other people.

We as humans have developed and so we must make sure that we don’t look at ourselves as stagnant in our social structure meaning that inclusive design will always change throughout the years and it is up to us as designers to act accordingly.
