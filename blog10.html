Week 10 - Decolonising Technology / CSS

We got assignment 2 this week the essay and I wanted to take my time to write a good essay that could get me some good marks so hopefully that goes well.

This week we did decolonisation of technology, and it was an interesting topic, to say the least as when looking at what it is we explore interesting concepts and things. To decolonise something means to free it from control so that everyone may participate and use that thing to their benefit so to decolonise technology means that we are allowing it to be used by all so that no one part controls it completely.

I liked the lecture and how we talked about how programmers are stereotyped and believed to look a certain way and act a certain way. In the past, it was certainly semi-true but nowadays the stereotypes are false this sector has developed leaps and bounds and so much more has been added to it. This sector is still believed to mainly be controlled by White males, but we can see that the sector is expanding, and the inclusion of other parties are slowly growing even though it is still mainly dominated by white males. To decolonise this, we open information and deconstruct it to see the truth behind the phenomena and allow others to explore this sector and become a part of it possibly evolving it to a new level and making something new of it.

I really liked that we talked about Silicon Valley and what is represents in terms of the hierarchy of the technological sector. We see that place and we can mainly think of white males that work there and that govern that sphere of activity so as we delve deeper, we can see how this can be fixed and changed to make a more diverse sector that allows ideas and people to flourish.
