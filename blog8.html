BLOG POST 8

Week 8 - The World in www / Semantic Markup

When reading “The World in WWW” I was really confused at first but then my mind travelled to how people use the internet to spread fake news and how the internet can be used to control the population as many people use it as their main means of news and communication. When thinking about that one realises just how dangerous the internet is if it was used as a tool for war or misinformation and it already happens often where content creators will talk about news and help spread misinformation.

It's crazy when one thinks about how fast the internet is at spreading information and fooling the public. When I think about it, I realised that due to the convenience and power of the internet, it can really be used nefariously. As the rate it produces information and controls information is completely governed by a party that we don’t really know about. We as people simply use the internet and mostly believe what it tells us even if its information is false.

When looking at the reading of geographies one also realised how the internet isn’t necessarily a resource that can be used everywhere and in a certain way can be viewed as fleeting. Especially when one realises that digital information isn’t physical and if we don’t have electricity or a way to access the internet, that would mean that the world’s information network has simply disappeared. Which is terrifying considering how we store a lot of information digitally.
