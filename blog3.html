Week 3 - Interaction and the www / CSS Fundamentals

so far, my experience in web development is going well. I decided to look into concepts that I would like to start aiming the website at so basically, I am choosing a theme. so far, I have a gym theme as well as the theme of a game that has me pretty excited and I think that they will look cool so I'm deciding between the two. the wireframing is going well and I am thinking of using my old website as the framework for the new one.

I have personally never been a fan of UI and UX as I can never seem to do it right or at least do it as well as I think it should be. what I do love about UI and UX is the thought processes behind it as it usually takes a lot of consideration and preparation as the developer must make it so that the website feels good and feels very pleasing to the eye as well as being simple to use and understand by the user. this is always interesting because you realise how many aspects one has to think of and design to make the website as good as it can be. the lectures tie well into this by explaining Ui and UX ad the reading supports my understanding of the conventions and uses of UI and UX.

seeing different lectures and having a variety of content to learn from is helpful and if feels better to have a broader base to choose form to build understanding in IM.
